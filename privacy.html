🍬 Privacy Policy

Mass Interactive Games built the Candy Mass game as a free game. This page informs you of our policies regarding collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Information Collection

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This is only used to save your game progress (score, level, settings) and to display your name on leaderboards. We do not share this data with third parties.

Guest Users

If you play as a guest, no personal data is collected. Your game progress is saved locally in your browser.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions, email us at: contact@massgms.com
